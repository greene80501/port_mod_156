About Me

Hi, I'm Wyatt Greene — a high school junior and aspiring developer studying Computer Information Systems at Front Range Community College. I first got into tech through my love of hardware and hands-on engineering, but it didn't take long before I was hooked on coding. One of my favorite memories is working on an ASL bot with a friend. Our camera wasn't working, so we had to manually feed it data — in the middle of a dead-silent class. When it finally returned the right letter, we jumped out of our chairs and yelled. That moment sealed it for me: This is what I love to do.

I'm passionate about AI, quantum computing, web development, and cybersecurity — in that order. I work mostly in Python, but I also use HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C, and SQL. I've taught a class on data science with Python, got into the iSAT program, and I'm constantly pushing myself to learn more. My dream is to develop the first AI that runs on a quantum computer — to build tech that impacts everyone and leaves a real mark on the world.
